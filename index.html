Project background

This project attempts to analyse a dataset obtained from Kaggle, specifically focusing on the distribution of global Powerplants, and present the various information within in an easier to read visual format.

Dataset used:

Link to the Dataset used

The dataset used for this project displays powerplants around the globe, the type of fuels they are operating, commissioning year, and estimated electrical production.

Dataset documentation

Documentation provided by the creators of the Dataset.

Documentation from the creators of the Dataset provided with it, explaining the methodology used in its collection and production and potential flaws of the dataset. For example, as the dataset is created from publically avaiable information, one of the issues raised in the documentation raises is that some types of renewable electrical Generation may be undercounted in the dataset, due to underreporting of newer and smaller facilites, such as solar and wind. This leads to these types of fuels potentially having lower counts within the dataset. In addition, as another example, certain data, such actual electrical generation figures, are rarely actually reported by the plants, meaning these figures are more from estimates made by the authors of the dataset instead.

Defintions of the categories used within the Dataset

Country
The Country that the Powerplant is located within
Powerplant Name
The name of the Powerplant
GPPD Identifier
A 10 or 12 character identifier for the powerplant within the database
Capacity
Electrical generating capacity in megawatts
Latitude
Geolocation in decimal degrees
Longitude
Geolocation in decimal degrees
Primary Fuel
Main energy source used in electrical generation by the powerplant
Other Fuel 1
Additional energy source used by a number of powerplants for electrical generation
Other Fuel 2
Additional energy source used by a number of powerplants for electrical generation
Commissioning Year
The year the powerplant first generated electricity
Owner
The majority shareholder of the powerplant, generally in a romanized format
Source
The entity that reported the data used to construct the dataset, generally in a romanized form
URL
URL linking to the source of the data where possible
Geolocation Source
The method used to gelocate the powerplant for the Latitude/Longitude
Year of Capacity Data
The Capacity of the powerplant's reported capacity
Year of Generation Data
Generation reported over the course of a year; many of the powerplants included in the dataset have entries over the 2013-2017 period
